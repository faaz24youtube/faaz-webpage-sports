Cricket

A game played with a ball and bat by two sides of usually 11 players each on a large field centering upon two wickets each defended by a batsman.

Football

Any of several games played between two teams on a usually rectangular field having goalposts or goals at each end and whose object is to get the ball over a goal line, into a goal, or between goalposts by running, passing, or kicking.

Hockey

Hockey is a game played on ice between two teams of 11 players who use long curved sticks to hit a small rubber disk, called a puck, and try to score goals.
